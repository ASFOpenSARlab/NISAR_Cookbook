Set Up an Earthdata Login Account for NISAR Data Access


You must have an Earthdata Login account and accept the Alaska Satellite Facility Data Access EULA to access NISAR data.

Create an Earthdata Login Account¶

  1. Navigate to Earthdata Login: https://urs.earthdata.nasa.gov/

  2. Click the REGISTER FOR A PROFILE button.

  1. Complete the registration form to create an account.

  2. Click the emailed link to verify your email address.

  3. Log into your new Earthdata Login account

Accept the Alaska Satellite Facility Data Access EULA¶

  1. Navigate to the EULAs tab in your Earthdata Login account.

  2. Click the Accept New EULAs link.

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the EULA list, find the Alaska Satellite Facility Data Access entry and click its ACCEPT EULA button.

  1. A EULA will appear. Read it and scroll to the bottom. Agree to the terms by clicking the checkbox.

  2. Click the AGREE button.
